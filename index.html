about the Beatles


The Beatles were an English rock band formed in Liverpool in 1960, and played during the sixties. The members were John Lennon (Guitarist), Paul McCartney (bassist), George Harrison (main Guitarist), and Ringo Starr (drummer).
The band, mainly Lennon&McCartney, were create many of the greatest songs in the word-"Hey Jude","Let It Be", "Yesterday", "Here Comes the Sun"...
The band performed in thousends of shows, in the "Beatlmania" period in the early 60s. The members couldn't stand in these crazy groopies, so they stopped their shows in 1965, and focused just on records until 1970.
After the band broked up, the members started a solo career, with some amazing songs like Lennon's "Imagine" or McCartney's "Live and Let Die".
